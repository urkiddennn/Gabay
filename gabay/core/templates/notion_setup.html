Notion Integration

Connect your Notion Workspace to Gabay Search.

How to connect:
  1. Go to notion.so/my-integrations.
  2. Create an Internal Integration and copy the Token.
  3. Open your Notion Database as a full page.
  4. The Database ID is the part of the URL after your workspace name and before the `?v=`.
  5. Crucial: Click "..." on your Database page -> "Add connections" -> Select your Integration.
